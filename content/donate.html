Donate to the GSB Project

Many aspects of developing and supporting GSB costs considerable amounts of time, but moreover, money. The two main mirrors, hosting the shell, web, and email accounts, source code reposiitory, the various software used in the development environment, and more, are paid out of our own pockets with our own hard-earned money.

Please consider donating funds to help support the GSB project. Donations are facilitated by a service called "Pledgie" (endorsed and used by the folks who host our source code repository). They accept many forms of payment, including PayPal.

We sincerely appreciate all you can do to help fund this project which allows us to give back to the users of Slackware.
