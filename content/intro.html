GNOME SlackBuild (GSB) - a GNOME distribution for Slackware Linux

GNOME SlackBuild v is a complete GNOME Desktop Environment for Slackware Linux v, encompassing all the needed packages for a full, clean installation of GNOME for Slackware. It aims to provide a fully functional desktop, including a wide range of multimedia, graphics, and development tools, as well as a complete networking and office suite.
